10. TOLONG BANTU DONGG PLEASEEEEEEE MAU DIKUMPULKAN BESOK, BUATKAN CERITA B INGGRIS YANG MENGANDUNG 16 TENSES, TRIMAKASIII​


Jawaban:

Dahulu kala, di sebuah desa yang tenang, hidup seorang gadis muda bernama Emily. Dia selalu bermimpi untuk menjelajahi dunia, dan suatu hari, dia memutuskan untuk meninggalkan rumahnya dan memulai petualangan.

Ketika Emily berjalan di sepanjang jalan berliku, dia menemukan peta misterius yang menjanjikan harta karun tersembunyi. Dengan penuh semangat, dia memutuskan untuk mengikuti peta itu. Sepanjang perjalanan, dia bertemu dengan seorang lelaki tua yang bijak, yang menceritakan pengalaman perjalanannya sendiri dan memberinya beberapa nasihat berharga.

Emily melanjutkan perjalanannya, menjelajahi pemandangan indah. Suatu hari, saat dia berkemah di alam bebas, dia menemukan bunga langka yang belum pernah dilihat sebelumnya. Dia tahu bahwa dia telah menemukan sesuatu yang luar biasa dan merasa bangga.

Dengan penemuan baru ini, Emily memutuskan untuk kembali ke desanya. Dia berbagi ceritanya yang menakjubkan dan memutuskan untuk menulis buku tentang petualangannya. Ketika dia menerbitkan bukunya, itu menjadi buku terlaris.

Petualangan Emily telah mengubah hidupnya selamanya, dan dia terus menginspirasi orang lain untuk mengikuti impian mereka. Ceritanya menjadi legenda yang masih diperbincangkan orang hingga hari ini.

Jawaban:

Once upon a time in a small village, there lived a girl named Sarah. She was a remarkable person who constantly amazed her neighbors.

Present Simple: Sarah lives in a small village.

Present Continuous: She is amazing her neighbors.

Sarah has a special ability to predict the weather.

Present Perfect Simple: She has a special ability.

Present Perfect Continuous: She has been predicting the weather for years.

One sunny morning, Sarah woke up and looked out of her window.

Past Simple: She woke up.

Past Continuous: She was looking out of her window.

She realized something strange was happening.

Past Perfect Simple: She had realized something strange.

Sarah quickly called her neighbors and told them about the approaching storm.

Past Simple: She called her neighbors.

Past Continuous: She was telling them about the storm.

As the storm raged on, Sarah's predictions proved to be accurate.

Past Simple: The storm raged on.

Past Perfect Continuous: Sarah's predictions had been proving accurate.

Sarah will always be remembered in her village for her incredible gift.

Future Simple: She will be remembered.

Future Continuous: Her gift will always be remembered.

She will have a special place in their hearts.

Future Perfect Simple: She will have a special place.

Future Perfect Continuous: She will have been remembered for her gift.

21. tuliskan 16 tenses dan waktunya!!!!


1. Present Tense  

Keterangan waktunya : always, often, every ( day, week, month), usually, sometimes, now and then, seldom, never, when, tomorrow, tonight, this morning, next year, next day dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Verb 1 + es / s + Predikat + Objek

I always wake up in the morning.  :Saya selalu bangun di pagi hari.

2. Present Continous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : now, right now, at present, today, at this moment, when, still, soon dan lainnya.

Rumus : subject + to be (am, is, are) + Verb – ing + Predikat + Objek

She is washing her hair now.

Dia (perempuan) sedang mencuci rambutnya sekarang

3. Present Perfect Tense

Keterangan waktunya : already, just, yet, so far, lately, never, ever, once, twice, several times, three times dan lainnya.

Rumus : Subject + Have/has + verb 3 + Predikat + Objek

I have gone to Bali island for three times.

Saya sudah pernah pergi ke pulau Bali 3 kali.

4. Present Perfect Continous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : for, since, long, how long, all the morning dan lainnya.

Rumus : Subject + has / have + been + verb-ing + Predikat + Objek

The passengers have been waiting for the next flight since this morning.

Para penumpang telah menunggu penerbangan berikutnya sejak pagi ini.  

5. Past Tense

Keterangan waktunya : yesterday, the day before, ago, last week, last month dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Verb2 + Predikat + Objek

She crossed over the bridge by motorcycle last night.

Dia ( perempuan ) menyebrangi jembatan dengan sepeda motor kemarin malam.

6. Past Continuous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : all day yesterday, the whole day yesterday, at … o clock yesteday dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + to be (was/were) + verb-ing + Predikat + Objek

I was praying when you came to my house yesterday.

Aku sedang beribadah ketika kamu datang ke rumahku kemarin.

7. Past Perfect Tense

Keterangan waktunya : by the end of …., before, after, as soon as, until dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Had + Verb III + Predikat + Objek

Sinta ad studied English before she came to my house.

Sinta sudah belajar Bahasa Inggris sebelum dia datang ke rumahku.

8. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Keterangan waktu yang dipakai: when, by last, by yesterday dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subject + had been + Verb-ing + Predikat + Objek

She had been reading eight story books since last month.

Dia ( perempuan )telah sedang membaca delapan buku cerita semenjak bulan lalu.  

9. Future Tense

Keterangan waktunya : tomorrow, morning, afternoon, evening, next …, tonight, soon, later,  dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + will + Verb 1 + Predikat + Objek

They will arrive from Japan next week.

Mereka akan tiba dari jepang minggu depan.

10. Future Continuous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : tomorrow, next …, at the same time, at … o’clock tomorrow, by this time dan sebaginya.

Rumus : Subjek + Shall/will + be + Verb-ing + Predikat + Objek |

We will be studying if you will come at seven o’clock p.m tomorrow.

Kami akan sedang belajar jika kamu datang pada jam tujuh malam besok.

11. Future Perfect Tense

Keterangan waktunya : by the of…, before, by now, by next, this time tomorrow dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + shall/will + have + Verb III + Predikat + Objek.

He will have married with Jesi by the and of this week.

Dia ( laki – laki )akan sudah menikah dengan Jesi menjelang akhir minggu ini.

12. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : by next …, by … o’clock, by this time tomorrow dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Shall/will + Have been + Verb-ing + Predikat + Objek

Father will have been building the house for a month on next week.

Ayah akan sudah sedang membangun rumah selama satu tahun pada minggu depan.

13. Future Past Tense

Keterangan waktunya : the day before, the previous day, the year before, when dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Should/would + V-1 + Predikat = Objek

He would buy magazine the previous day.

Dia ( Laki – laki ) akan membeli majalah hari sebelumnya.

14. Future Past Continuous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : at … o’clock yesteday, on  ( month ) last year, tomorrow, the day before dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Should/Would + Be + V-ing + Predikat + Objek

We should be waiting for the next two hours.

Kita harus menunggu dua jam berikutnya.

15. Future Past Perfect

Keterangan waktunya : at yesterday, on last week, in last year dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + Should/Would + Have + V-3 + Predikat + Objek

I should have worked in this office last night.

Seharusnya aku bekerja di kantor ini tadi malam.

16. Future Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Keterangan waktunya : by the end of this …, by next month, by the next year dan sebagainya.

Rumus : Subjek + should/would + have + been + verb-ing + Predikat + Objek

I would have been building a wall for three days.

Saya dulu pernah berencana akan terus membangun dinding selama 3 hari

1. Simple Present ( Subject + v1 )
untuk menjelaskan kejadian/fakta yang sedang terjadi
2. Present Continuous ( S+ is/am/are + present participle/v-ing)
untuk membicarakan aksi yg sedang terjadi atau rencana untuk masa depan
3. Present Perfect (S+ has/have + v3)
untuk menjelaskan kejadian yang sudah berlalu, baik sudah selesai maupun masih terjadi
4. Present Perfect Continuous (s+has/have+been+v-ing)
mirip mirip present perfect
5. Simple Past ( S+v2 )
untuk menjelaskan kejadian yang sudah terjadi dimasa lalu
6. Past continuous (s+was/were+v-ing)
untuk menjelaskan aksi yang sedang terjadi di masa lampau
7. Past Perfect (S+had+v3)
untuk menjelaskan sesuatu yang sudah selesai pada waktu tertentu lalu aksi sebelum "penyelesaian" itu terjadi
contoh: When he came last night, the cake had run out
8. Past Perfect Continuous (S+had+been+v-ing)
untuk menjelaskan kejadian yang sudah selesai pada masa tertentu
9. Simple Future (S+will+bare infinitive) (S+be+going to+bare infinitive)
untuk meramalkan apa yang akan terjadi di masa depan secara spontan

menjelaskan sesuatu yang akan terjadi dimasa depan pada waktu tertentu
11. Future Perfect Continuous (S+will+have+been+v-ing)
menjelaskan tentang kejadian yang sedang terjadi, dan akan masih berlanjut sampai masa depan di waktu tertentu
12. Simple Past Future (S+would+bare infinitive)
untuk membuat janji untuk masa depan
13. Past future continuous (S+would+be+present participle)
menjelaskan suatu situasi imajiner, tetapi kita tidak ada di "tempat imajiner"
contoh: I would be attending the conference if i'm in Jakarta (tapi tidak sedang di Jakarta)
14. Past Future Perfect (s+would+have+v3)
menjelaskan aksi yang tidak terjadi di masa lalu
contoh bhs. indo: kalau saja perhiasanmu kau simpan di deposit box, pasti itu tidak hilang
15. Past future perfect continuous (S+would+have+been+present participle)
mejelaskan situasi imajiner pada waktu tertentu
16. Future Perfect (S+will+have+v3)
menjelaskan aktivitas akan selesai pada masa tertentu di masa depan
contoh indo: bulan depan aku akan menyelesaikan kursus matematikaku

Past tense,present tense,past perfect tense,present perfect tense,present continouse tense,past continouse tense,future,present perfect continouse,past perfect continouse.sudah,itu saja yang saya tahu,maaf jika ada kesalahan Simple Present
(+) S + V1 (+es/s)                    e.g: Sun Rises from east (matahari terbit dari timur).
(-) S + Do/Does + not +V1        e.g: I don’t know where to go.
(?) Do/Does + not + S + V1?    e.g: Do you speak English (apakah kamu berbahasa Inggris?)

Present Continuous.
(+) S + am/is/are + Ving               e.g: I am going to your home tomorrow
(-)  S + am/is/are + not + Ving    e.g: I am not crying
(?) Am/is/are + S + Ving?             e.g: Are you still waiting for some one?

Present Perfect
(+) S + have/has + V3                e.g: Peter has gone just now.
(-)  S + have/has + not + V3      e.g: I have not done my home work yet.
(?) Have/has + S + V3?              e.g: Have you already taken a bath?

Present Perfect Continuous
(+) S + have/has + been + Ving              e.g: It has been raining since at six o’clock
(-)  S + have/has + not + been + Ving    e.g:I haven’t been eating for whole day
(?) Have/has + S + been + Ving               e.g: Has she been waiting for me for a long this time?

Simple Past.
(+) S + V2 + O                          e.g: I went to school alone yesterday.
(-)  S + did + not + Inf (V1)     e.g: You didn’t tell that you took my money yesterday.
(?) Did + S + Inf (V1)                e.g: Did you came home last year?

Past Continuous.
(+) S + was/were + Ving              e.g: I was sleeping when you came.
(-)  S + was/were + not + Ving  e.g: You weren’t listening to me when I was singing
(?)  Was/were + S + Ving            e.g: Was she still reading while father called her?

Past Perfect.
(+) S + Had + V3             e.g: She had gone before you came
(-)  S + Had + not + V3  e.g: I hadn’t already taken a bath when you visit me
(?)  Had + S + V3            e.g: had you already graduated from senior high school last year?

Past Perfect Continuous.
(+) S + had + been + Ving     e.g: Rooney had been playing football for 3 years till he decided to stop last year.
(-)  S+had+not+been+Ving  e.g: I had not learning English for a year at 2010
(?) had+S+been+Ving           e.g: Had father been working at PT.Astra for 10 years last year?

Simple Future.
(+) S + will + Inf (V1)             e.g: I will always love you if you love me to
(-)  S + will+ not + Inf (V1)   e.g: I won’t let you go
(?) Will + S + Inf (V1)             e.g: will you come to my party?

Future Continuous.
(+) S + will + be + Ving           e.g: he will be climbing Krakatau with her friends on sunday
(-)  S+ will + not + be + Ving  e.g: I wont be traveling next week.
(?)  Will + S + be + Ving           e.g: will you be still sleeping at 7 a.m tomorrow morning?

Future Perfect.
(+) S + will + have + V3             e.g: he will have arrived by the end of this month
(-)  S + will + have + not + V3  e.g: he will not have slept at the time you go home.
(?) Will + S + have + V3             e.g: will you have taken bath at 4 pm?

Future Perfect Continuous.
(+) S+will+have+been+Ving,          e.g: he will have been working for 3 hours at 9 am tomorrow.
(-)  S+will+have+not+been+Ving,  e.g: she will have not been traveling for 3 weeks next sunday.
(?) Will+S+have+been+Ving,          e.g: will you have been diving in the sea for 3 hours at mid day?

Past Future.
(+): S + would + Infinitive/V1,           e.g: I would have gone if you didn’t prevent me.
(-): S + would + not + Infinitive/V1,  e.g: You would not come to your party yesterday, whatever happened.
(?): Would + S + Infinitive/V1,            e.g: would you attend my wedding party if I invited you?

Past Future Continuous.
(+) S + would + be + Ving              e.g: When I was born, He would be working at this company for 2 years
(-)  S + would + not + be + Ving    e.g: You would not be walking alone, if I were not sick.
(?) Would + S + be + Ving               e.g: would you be standing by me, if I slept yesterday?

Past Future Perfect.
(+) S + would + have + been + V3             e.g: This house would have been painted if all workers had came yesterday.
(-)  S + would + not + have + been + V3   e.g: It would not have been finished by the end of last month.
(?) Would +S+ have+ been+ V3                  e.g: Would he have been delivered all my orders if I had paid my bill?

Past Future Perfect Continuous.
(+) S + would + have + been + V-ing             e.g: You would have been sleeping
(-) S + would + not + have + been + V-ing   e.g: She would not have been crying
(?) Would + S + have + been + V-ing?          e.g: Would you have living at London for 3 years last year?
