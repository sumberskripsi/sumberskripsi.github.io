2. buatlah soal past tense + past perfect (5),past tense + past continous(5)


A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Note :
Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".
Baik PAST TENSE dan PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

B. PAST TENSE VS PAST PERFECT
1. When she was 21 years old, she had married
2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old
3. They had had lunch, before they went to the library
4. Before we watched television, we had had done our homework
5. After they had gone to Paris, they went to England

Note :
Gunakan PAST PERFECT pada kalimat atau kejadian yang sudah selesai dikerjakan, dan dilanjutkan kejadian selanjutnya dimana kalimatnya menggunakan SIMPLE PAST. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, PAST PERFECT bisa diartikan ".... SUDAH MELAKUKAN".

17. Buatlah - past continue (5) - past tense (5) - present tense (5) - past tense + while + past continue - past continue + when + past tense - while * past continue + past tense - when + past tende + past continue


PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINOUS  Jawaban    Pendahuluan  

Simple past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung di waktu lampau. Keterangan waktu yang menunjukan waktu lampau dibutuhkan disini, seperti: yesterday, last week, two days ago, two hours ago, last night, dan lain lain.

Rumus simple past adalah:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

Past Continuous Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung dan mempunyai proses di masa lampau. Disini penggunaan kata when dan while sering digunakan ketika past continuous tense bertembu simple past tense.  

Rumus Past continuous adalah:

(+) S + was/were + Ving + O

(-) S + was/were not + Ving + O

(?) Was/were + S + Ving + O?

was = I, He, She, It

were = You, They, We

Pembahasan  

 Buatlah kalimat:

Past Continuous:

I was watching TV at the same time yesterday.Mrs. Susan was teaching us yesterday at 9 am.They were playing football here yesterday at 3 pmWe were visiting Tina yesterday afternoon.My father was working at the same time yesterday.

Simple Past Tense

I bought a new car last monthWe won the game yesterday.They were the champion of Indonesian League last season.Rina visited her grandmother last week.He was the victim of earthquake in Palu.

Simple Present Tense

I always go to school at 6.30 amThe sun rises from the east.Where do you come from?I don't know what you mean.She plays piano beautifully.

Past Continuous + while + Past Continuous

My father was washing his car while my mother was sweeping the floor.

Past Continuous + when + past tense

I was playing video game when you came.

While + Past Continuous + Past Tense

While I was daydreaming in the class, Mrs. Susan called my name.

When + Past Tense + Past Continuous

When my mother knocked the door, I was sleeping.

Simple past tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa lampau

future tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

rumus:

simple past

+ ) s + v2+ O.

-) s + did + v1 + O

?) did + s +v1 + O ?.

simple future

+ ) s + will/shall +v1 + O

- ) s + will/shall+not+v1+O

? ) will/shall+s+v1+O ?.

contoh :

simple past tense

+ ) i ate rice , yesterday( saya makan nasi kemarin)

-) i did eat rice, yesterday(saya tidak makan nasi kemarin)

? ) did i eat rice,yesterday ?.(apakah saya makan nasi kemarin?)

simple future

+ ) i will eat rice tomorrow ( aku akan makan nasi besok)

- ) i wil not eat rice tomorrow(aku tidak akan makan nasi besok)

? ) will i eat rice tomorrow ?.(akankan aku makan nasi besok?)

note: untuk penggunaan "will" ketika subjek kalimat berbentuk (i,you,we,they,she,he,it,dan nama orang lebih dari satu nama ex: doni and riza)

untuk penggunaan shall (I,she,he,it,dan nama orang tunggal ex : doni )
