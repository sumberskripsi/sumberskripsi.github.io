5. guys ini soal TOEFL structure, tolong dibantu yah​


1. Marmots spend their time foraging among meadow plants and flowers or sunning on rocky cliff. (pilihan jawaban B)

2. The greenhouse effect occurs when the Earth's atmosphere traps heat radiated from the sun. (pilihan jawaban C)

3. The Rose Bowl, which takes place on New York's Day, is the oldest postseason collegiate football game in the United States. (pilihan jawaban C)

_________ExplanationNOMOR 1 ↓

Untuk membentuk kalimat/klausa dalam Bahasa Inggris, penting untuk memerhatikan keparalelan (kesetaraan) bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan. Misalnya:

We studied English and did our homework last night. (kata kerja studied dan did adalah pararel karena bentuknya sama, yaitu menggunakan bentuk Verb 2)I love drawing, skiing, and writing. ("kata kerja" atau lebih tepatnya kata benda bentuk ing yang digunakan dalam kalimat adalah paralel, yaitu menggunakan bentuk Verb-ing)To learn English and to live longer are my lifetime ambition. ("kata kerja" atau lebih tepatnya subjek yang digunakan dalam kalimat adalah paralel, yaitu menggunakan bentuk To-infinitive)

     Apabila kita melihat struktur kalimat soal nomor 1, sebenarnya merupakan bentuk singkat dari kalimat berikut ini.

"Marmots spend their time while they are foraging among meadow plants and flowers or they are sunning on rocky cliffs."

     Kalimat di atas terdiri dari 3 klausa, yaitu:

Marmots spend their time.They are foraging among meadow plants and flowers.They are sunning on rocky cliffs.

     Ketiga klausa di atas disambung menjadi satu kalimat dengan konjungsi while dan or.

     Konjungsi seperti or dapat digunakan untuk menghubungkan dua klausa yang setara.

     Karena klausa kedua dan ketiga setara, maka kata kerja yang digunakan pun bentuknya sama, yaitu menggunakan bentuk Verb-ing.

NOMOR 2 ↓

When merupakan salah satu jenis konjungsi. Ingat! Konjungsi/Kata penghubung adalah salah satu kelompok kelas kata dalam Bahasa Inggris; digunakan untuk menghubungkan beberapa kata, frasa, dan klausa menjadi satu kalimat.

     Kalimat di soal nomor 2 terdiri atas 2 klausa, yaitu:

The greenhouse effect occurs.The Earth's atmosphere traps heat radiated from the Sun. (Ingat! radiated dalam klausa kedua adalah bentuk singkat dari that is radiated)

     Kedua klausa di atas digabungkan dengan sebuah konjungsi, yaitu when.

Konjungsi kata tanya, walaupun juga digunakan dalam kalimat tanya, tidak akan memberi pengaruh terhadap bentuk klausa dalam kalimat tanya tidak langsung. Misalnya:

When will I give birth? (kalimat nomor 1 adalah contoh kalimat tanya, strukturnya adalah Question word + Auxiliary verb + Subject + Verb)I don't know when I will give birth. (kalimat nomor 2 adalah contoh kalimat tanya tidak langsung, strukturnya adalah Clause 1 + Question word (Conjunction) + Clause 2)

Karena di kalimat soal nomor 2 kata when berfungsi sebagai konjungsi, maka struktur yang digunakan oleh kalimat soal nomor 2 adalah struktur kalimat tanya tidak langsung.

NOMOR 3 ↓

Terdapat dua Relative Pronouns yang artinya sama, yaitu which dan that. Yang berbeda dari kedua kata itu adalah cara menggunakannya.

     Which digunakan untuk memberi karakteristik/sifat tambahan pada subjek/objek. Sementara itu, That digunakan untuk memberi batasan karakteristik/sifat pada subjek/objek. Misalnya:

The big house, which is actually not that big, is mine. [Rumah besar itu, yang sebenarnya ga besar-besar amat, adalah milikku]The big house that is actually not that big is mine. [Rumah besar yang sebenarnya ga besar-besar amat itu adalah milikku]

     Sering sekali apabila digunakan untuk memberi tambahan karakteristik/sifat pada subjek, kata which beserta kata seterusnya diletakkan dalam tempat yang dibatasi oleh dua koma. Strukturnya seperti berikut ini.

Subject, which ...., + Verb

atau

Object, which ...., + Clause

     Dalam kalimat di soal nomor 3, yaitu place on New Year's Day diletakkan dalam tempat yang dibatasi oleh dua koma; merupakan tambahan karakteristik/sifat pada subjek The Rose Bowl.

     Oleh karena itu, kita dapat mengambil kesimpulan bahwa kata yang tepat untuk mengisi titik-titik di soal haruslah mengandung kata which. Dengan demikian, jawaban yang tepat adalah pilihan jawaban C.

22. write down the characteristics and generic structure of recount textkk bantu pls, dikumpul bsk soalnya​


Characteristics of a Recount Text:

1. Past Tense: Recount texts are typically written in the past tense as they recount events or experiences that have already occurred.

2. Chronological Order: Recount texts follow a chronological order, presenting events or actions in the order in which they happened. This helps to create a clear and organized structure.

3. First-person or Third-person Point of View: Recount texts can be written from either a first-person perspective (using pronouns like "I" or "we") or a third-person perspective (using pronouns like "he," "she," or "they").

4. Time Connectors: Time connectors are commonly used in recount texts to indicate the sequence of events. Examples of time connectors include "first," "next," "then," "afterward," "finally," and "in the end."

5. Descriptive Language: Recount texts often incorporate descriptive language to provide details and enhance the reader's understanding of the events or experiences being recounted.

Generic Structure of a Recount Text:

1. Orientation: The orientation sets the scene by providing background information about the time, place, people, or context of the recounted events. It answers questions such as "Who?" and "When?" and provides a general introduction to the topic.

2. Events: This section presents the main events or actions that occurred. It follows a chronological order, providing a step-by-step account of what happened. Time connectors are used to indicate the sequence of events.

3. Reorientation (optional): In some recount texts, a reorientation section may be included. This section summarizes the events or reflects on their significance. It may include personal opinions, feelings, or reflections on the experience.

Note: The generic structure of a recount text can vary depending on the purpose and context. Some recount texts may include additional sections like a complication or resolution, particularly in narratives or personal recounts.


23. pengertian social function generic structure dan langguage feature​tolong dong bantu hari ini di kumpul....


Jawaban:

Function :

• Report text is a text which present information about something, as it is. It is as a result of systematic observation and analyses.

• Its social purpose is presenting information about something. They generally describe an entire class of things, whether natural or made: mammals, the planets, rocks, plants, countries of region, culture, transportation, and so on.

Generic Structure of Report Text :

• General Classification: General statements that describe the subject of the report, description, and classification.

• Description: tells what the phenomenon under discussion ; in terms of parts, qualities, habits or behaviors.

Language features of Report text :

• Nouns and noun phrases are used rather than personal pronouns. The use of personal pronouns is limited.

• Most reports are written in the present tense.

• Use some passive voice

• Some reports use technical or scientific terms.

• Linking verbs are used, eg. is, are, has, have, belong to, to give coherence.

• Uses some action verbs (climb, eat).

• Descriptive language is used that is factual rather than imaginative eg. color, shape, size, body parts, habits, behaviors, functions, uses.

Example of Report Text :

A bicycle, is called a bike or cycle, is a human-powered, pedal-driven, single-track vehicle, having two wheels attached to a frame, one behind the other. A bicycle rider is called a cyclist, or bicyclist.

Bicycles were introduced in the 19th century in Europe and as of 2003, more than 1 billion have been produced worldwide, twice as many as the number of automobiles that have been produced. They are the principal means of transportation in many regions. They also provide a popular form of recreation, and have been adapted for use as children's toys, general fitness, military and police applications, courier services, and bicycle racing.

31. write generic structure (text structure) of news item text!


Generic structures of News Item text :

Main Event / Newsworthy EventBackground Event / ElaborationSource

Pembahasan

News item is a kind of text that has a purpose to inform readers / listeners about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important.

Generic structures :

A. Main event / newsworthy event

=> It is the main information. It explains the summary of an important event happens.

B. Background event / elaboration

=> It explains the the background of an event in details. Who are involved, where does it happen and how does it happen.

C. Source

=> It consists of the source of the news in the form of interviewees, the witnesses' comment, the expert's opinion, and the statement of involved subjects in an event.
