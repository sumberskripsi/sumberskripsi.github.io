13. soal-soal olimpiade matamatika sma dan caranya​


Jawaban:

diketahui bilangan bulat x dan y dibagi 4, maka bersisa 3.

x=4m+3

y=4n+3

3y=3(4n+3)=4(3n)+9=4(3n+2)+1

x-3y=4m+3-(4(3n+2)+1)

=4m+3-4(3n+2)-1

=4(m-3n-2)+2

jadi jika bilangan x-3y dibagi 4 maka bersisa 2

[tex](f\,o\,g)(x)=4x-1\\ g(x)=x^2+4x-2\\ f(x)=?[/tex]

[tex]y=x^2+4x-2[/tex]

[tex]x=-2+\sqrt{y+6}[/tex]

[tex]f(x^2+4x-2)=4x-1\\\\ f(y)=4(-2+\sqrt{y+6})-1\\\\ f(y)=-8+4\sqrt{y+6}-1\\\\ f(y)=4\sqrt{y+6}-9\\\\ f(x)=4\sqrt{x+6}-9[/tex]
