Kata baku adalah kata yang sesuai dengan kaidah kebahasaan. Kata baku di Indonesia merupakan kata yang menyesuaikan dengan KBBI (Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia). Kata baku merupakan kata yang benar dari penulisan dan kebahasaan.

Pembahasan

Ciri-Ciri Bahasa Baku

Kata baku merupakan kata nasional, tidak dipengaruhi kosakata bahasa lain. Kata baku tidak termasuk dalam bahasa gaul (bahasa kekinian). Bahasa baku tidak memiliki perubahan kata. Bahasa baku mengandung satu makna pasti. Kata baku sesuai kaidah kebahasaan KBBI. Kata baku ditulis dengan EYD. Bahasa baku ditulis dengan ringkas. Perbedaan Bahasa Baku dan Tidak Baku Kata baku ringkas, sedangkan kata tidak baku bertele-tele. Kata baku terkesan lebih sopan dari kata tidak baku. Bahasa baku mengandung satu makna, bahasa tidak baku mengandung lebih dari satu makna. Bahasa baku sesuai KBBI, sedangkan kata tidak baku berlawanan. Penyelesaian

Snow White

Once upon a time there lived a little girl named Snow White. She lived with her aunt and uncle because her parents were dead.

One day she heard her uncle and aunt talking about leaving Snow White in the castle because they both wanted to go to America and they didn’t have enough money to take Snow White.

Snow White didn’t want her uncle and aunt to do that so she decided that it would be best if she ran away. The next morning she ran away into the woods. She was very tired and hungry. Then she saw a little cottage. She knocked but no one answered so she went inside and fell asleep.

Meanwhile, the seven dwarfs were coming home from work. There they found Snow White sleeping. Then Snow White woke up. She saw the dwarfs.

The dwarfs said, “What is your name?”

Snow White said, “My name is Snow White.”

Then, Snow White told the dwarfs the whole story.

The dwarfs said, “If you want, you may live here with us.”

Snow White answered, “Oh, could I? Thank you.”

Finally, Snow White and the seven dwarfs lived happily ever after.

31. Soal narrative text pilihan ganda sma dan jawabannya


Narrative Text

One day in the forest which was still green and beautiful, the horse at that time still had horns. He proudly reflected in the clear lake in the middle of the forest while admiring his appearance with horns like a king's crown. At that time a deer appeared, which at that time had no horns, joined the mirror and drank in the lake. However, he was immediately chased away by the horse who felt that the deer only interfered with the view and interfered with the enjoyment. The deer sadly left.

Suddenly, from a distance, there was the sound of a tiger roaring loudly, as if to shake the entire dwellers of the quiet forest. The birds were startled flying, all the forest dwellers were hiding in fear. The horse and deer fled as fast as they could. The deer are agile and able to run among the bushes and tree branches. But unfortunately the horse horns caught in a tree branch and screamed for help. Nobody wants to help.

Suddenly, from the bush, unexpectedly a deer appeared, quietly nibbling on a tree branch that was wrapped around a horse's horn. The horse was touched and did not expect the deer's kindness.

When they finally got out of the danger, the horse found it helpful and presented its horns to the deer in gratitude. That's the origin of the deer having antlers.

Who still had horns at that time?

a. The deer

b. The horse

c. The tiger

d. The branch

Why did the horse chase away the deer?

a. He hated the deer.

b. The deer wasn’t pretty.

c. The deer disturbed his enjoyment.

d. The horse was sad and want to be alone.

Why did the horse and the deer run away?

a. they wanted to test who was the faster one.

b. the tiger chased them.

c. the tiger attacked them.

d. They were afraid of the roaring sound of the tiger.

Who finally came to help the horse?

a. The birds.

b. The tiger.

c. The deer.

d. The bush.

Where did the scene happen?

a. It happened in the bush.

b. It happened in a lake.

c. It happened in a green forest.

d. It happened in a zoo.

Pembahasan

Narrative text adalah teks yang menceritakan urutan kejadian yang dialami pelaku dalam teks tersebut, baik itu fiksi maupun non fiksi.

Dalam soal di atas yang dijadikan narrative text adalah cerita fiksi berbentuk fabel yang diperankan oleh binatang penghuni hutan. Dalam narrative text ada empat unsur, yaitu:

Orientation yang merupakan pengenalan awal cerita. Complication yang merupakan awal datangnya masalah. Resolution yaitu pemecahan masalah yang dialami oleh tokoh cerita. Re-orientation yaitu kesimpulan atas seluruh cerita atau berisi pesan cerita tersebut. Pelajari lebih lanjut
