12. bagian-bagian prosedur text


1. Goal ( judul )
2. materials / ingredients ( bahan bahan )
3. steps ( langkah - langkah )Generic structure:
Goal: merupakan tahap tujuan kegiatan yang ingin dilakukan
Materials: merupakan tahap yang berisi bahan- bahan yang digunakan untuk merelasikan goal

Steps: merupakan tahap yang berisi langkah- langkah dalam merealisikan

Language feature:
Mengunakan kalimat imperative misalnya: cut, don't mix,dsb
Mengunakan action verbs misalnya turn, put, dsb

Mengunakaan connectives untuk mengurutkan kegiatan

Misalnya:then, while, dsb,
Mengunakaan adverbial untuk menyatakaan rinci waktunya dan tempat , cara akurat, misalnya for five minutes, 2 centimeters from the top, dsb

17. kumpulan text narrative beserta soal dan jawaban


Narrative Text

In the forest the fastest running animal is the rabbit. No one can beat him. The rabbit arrogantly posed a challenge to all forest dwellers to be able to defeat him.

When none of the other animals dared to accept his challenge, suddenly the turtle, which was known to walk very slowly, said he had accepted the challenge. One forest in unison laughed at him in disbelief. Moreover, the rabbit who was so arrogant underestimated the ability of the turtle.

On the day of the start of the match the rabbit began to show his ability to run very fast at the beginning of the match. The turtle began to walk quietly and slowly, as if ignoring the ability and arrogance of the rabbit.

The rabbit because he felt that he had the upper hand after running a long distance, he walked leisurely, then sat under a tree. The very cool wind apparently lulled the rabbit to sleep.

Apparently the turtle although walking very slowly but he never stopped. Until finally he passed the rabbit who was asleep under a tree.

Finally the rabbit woke up and chased the turtle, but unfortunately his efforts were in vain, because the turtle had already reached the finish line and became the winner. The rabbit realized his arrogance.

The fable above gives a message to us that high ability if not accompanied by humility will only produce arrogance. On the other hand, ordinary abilities but accompanied by perseverance will definitely produce real results.

Questions

Who was the fastest animal in the forest?What was the rabbit’s challenge?Why did the all forest laugh at the turtle?What did the rabbit do under the tree?How did the turtle defeat the fast rabbit?What can you learn from the story?Pembahasan

The story above is a part of narrative text. You can find narrative text in many forms, including stories: fictions and non fictions, fables, or biographies.

The best way to learn a narrative text is by reading many stories in English then answering questions about the text. Or if there is no question you can create your own question to get a better comprehension of the text.

In the the text above we have created the following answers:

The fastest animal in the forest was the rabbit. The rabbit's challenge was anyone who can beat him in speed. The all forest laugh at the turtle because he wanted to beat the fast rabbit with his slow speed. The rabbit fell asleep under the tree. The turtle defeated the fast rabbit by his patience and perseverance. I can learn from the story that the patience and perseverance can beat arrogances and skill. Pelajari lebih lanjut

38. Soalnya tentang procedur text (how to insert sim card cellphone)


When inserting the SIM Card to cellphone, make sure that the cellphone has been switched off and follow the direction bellow :

1. First of all, press the locking catch and slide the cover then lift it off the phone.

2. After that, push two catches in the opposite directions and remove the battery.

3. Next, slide the SIM card carefully into the slot and make sure that the golden connect arson are facing to the connector of the phone.

4. Then, put the battery and align it until snaps into its place.

5. Finally, insert the two catches of the back cover corresponding slot in the phone and slide the cover forward button of the phone until locks into place

6. Don't forget to switch on the cellphone. Wait until it is ready to use
