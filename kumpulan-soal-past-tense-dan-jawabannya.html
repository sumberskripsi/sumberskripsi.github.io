9. buatlah soal past tense + past perfect (5),past tense + past continous(5)


A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Note :
Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".
Baik PAST TENSE dan PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

B. PAST TENSE VS PAST PERFECT
1. When she was 21 years old, she had married
2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old
3. They had had lunch, before they went to the library
4. Before we watched television, we had had done our homework
5. After they had gone to Paris, they went to England

Note :
Gunakan PAST PERFECT pada kalimat atau kejadian yang sudah selesai dikerjakan, dan dilanjutkan kejadian selanjutnya dimana kalimatnya menggunakan SIMPLE PAST. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, PAST PERFECT bisa diartikan ".... SUDAH MELAKUKAN".

37. soal dan jawaban tentang past tense vs present perfect


Subject : English  

Category :Tenses  

Sub-category : Present perfect tense

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

--------

Present perfect tense dan past simple tense adalah salah dua dari 16 tense dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Present perfect digunakan untuk kejadian yang dimulai pada suatu waktu yang tidak diketahui di masa lampau dan 'sempurna' di masa sekarang atau hasilnya terasa/terlihat/ada sekarang.

Konstruksi : S + have/has + past participle (v₃)

Penanda : already, yet, never, ever, since ..., for ..., just ..., ... times, ada jumlah, dll.

-----------

Past simple digunakan untuk kejadian yang terjadi dan selesai di suatu waktu spesifik di masa lampau.

Konstruksi : S + preterite (v₂)

Penanda : yesterday, last ..., ... ago, just now, this morning, dll.

--------

Contoh soal dan jawaban :

1. The Titanic ..............sank.................... (sink) in 1912.

2. Dolphins ............have lived................. (live) in BC waters for millions of years, and are still common today.

3. I ............wore...................... (wear) these boots to go hiking yesterday.

4. Jennie .........has never eaten............ (never, eat) bacon in her life! She's a true vegetarian.

5. I ..........have bought........ (buy) some new shorts, but I don't think I like them. Maybe I'll take them back to the store.

6. We .............have known............. (know) each other since we.......were.......... (be) children.

7. Peter ..........has called........ (call) you three times already. He ..........called........ (call) again just now.
