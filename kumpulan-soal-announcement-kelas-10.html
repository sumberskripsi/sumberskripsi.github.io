11. Buatlah 2 teks announcement dan buat 10 soal beserta jawaban


Understanding Announcement Text

Written announcement

STUDY TOUR TO PARAPAT

To all students of Grade IX SMPN 2 Medan

We are going to leave for Parapat tomorrow morning, February 18th 2019, at 7 a.m. Please, come at least 30 minutes before departure. Bring along your student ID card, mobile phone and small note.

For more information please contact Ms. Siti on 0811238482920

____________________________

Spoken announcement

Attention please to all the passengers.

We would like to inform you that a black suitcase cabin size under the brand TravelPro has been found unattended in the waiting room gate 25.

Please contact our information service for more detail about this. Thank you and have a safe flight.

Pembahasan

1. What is the first announcement about?

The first announcement is about study tour to Parapat for students of Grade IX SMPN 2 Medan.

2. What is the second announcement about?

The second announcement is about lost suitcase.

3. When students will leave to Parapat?

February 18th 2019, at 7 a.m.

4. When they should be at school?

At least 30 minutes before departure.

5. What students should bring?

Student ID card, mobile phone and small note.

6. Where can we hear the second announcement?

We can hear the announcement at the airport.

7. Where the suitcase was found?

In the waiting room gate 25.

8. What you should do if that’s your suitcase?

I should contact to the information center about it.

9. Do you think both of the announcement has similarity?

Yes, it’s the generic structure.

10. What do you prefer, written or spoken announcement?

I prefer written because sometimes we missed the spoken announcement due to hectic activity.
