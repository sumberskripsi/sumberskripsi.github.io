Penjelasan Lebih Lanjut

THERE

Kata 'there' digunakan untuk menunjukkan keberadaan sesuatu. Dalam terjemahan bebas bahasa Indonesia there berarti ada. Dalam penggunaannya 'there is' dan 'there are' ditentukan oleh berapa banyak benda (noun) yang disebutkan.

There is digunakan jika benda yang disebutkan hanya satu (tunggal)

There are digunakan jika benda yang disebutkan lebih dari satu (jamak)

Expressing of Checking Someone's Understanding

Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain:

Do you understand?

Do you understand what I mean?

Are you with me?

Do you know what I'm saying?

Expression of Asking for Attention

Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain

Attention, please!

May I have your attention, please?

Excuse me...

Stop talking please!

Can I have your attention, please?

Expression of Congratulation

Expression of congratulation adalah ekspresi yang biasa kita ucapkan ketika seseorang meraih keberhaslan atau mencapai sukses dalam meraih sesuatu atau melakukan sesuatu. Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain:

Congratulation!

Happy Birthday!

Best Wishes!

Happy wedding!

Happy anniversary

24. Make a conversation by using the expression and response of an appointment!


Answer:

Tiara: Good morning. Is this Mr. Adrian’s number?

Adrian: Yes, this is he. Who am I speaking to?

Tiara: Mr. Adrian, I’m Miss Tiara. You daughter’s teacher.

Adrian: Oh, of course, Miss Tiara. What can I do for you?

Tiara: I’m sorry to bother you, but I would like to arrange an appointment to discuss about Nabila.

Adrian: Is there anything wrong with Nabila, Miss?

Tiara: I won’t say there is. I’m merely concern over something and I think it’s best if we can talk directly.

Adrian: Sure. Do you have any time on your mind?

Tiara: I do. Would you be available on Friday afternoon after the class is over?

Adrian: This Friday? Oh, I’m terribly sorry, Miss Tiara, but I will be away by then through the weekend for a seminar out of town. But, wait, if it’s really urgent, I think I can wiggle some room for Friday morning.

Tiara: Hmm… Unfortunately I already have another appointment Friday morning, Mr. Adrian. Would you be free to meet early next week, though?

Adrian: I will free up my Monday for you.

Tiara : All right, then. Monday sounds fine. Shall we meet around 3.30 in the afternoon?

Adrian: Of course. Monday afternoon it is.

Tiara: Please, let me know if your plan changes and the time is not suitable for you.

Adrian: I will. I’m sorry for the inconvenience.

Tiara: Don’t worry, Mr. Adrian. Thank you and good morning.

Adrian: Good morning.

Explanation:

Points To Look For!

1. Make an Appointment

2. Agree on an Appointment

3. Claims That The Timing is Not Correct

4. Determine The Meeting Time

5. Cancel The Appointment as Well as Apologize

6. Confirm The Appointment

Sorry If Wrong:)
